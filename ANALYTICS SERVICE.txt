ANALYTICS SERVICE
=================

PHASE 1: BUSINESS EVENTS GENERATED
----------------------------------

1. User books ride (done)
   ↓
2. RideRequestedEvent
   ↓
3. Publish Kafka

4. Driver accepts ride (done)
   ↓
5. RideAcceptedEvent
   ↓
6. Publish Kafka

7. Ride starts
   ↓
8. RideStartedEvent (done)
   ↓
9. Publish Kafka

10. Ride completes
    ↓
11. RideCompletedEvent (done)
    ↓
12. Publish Kafka

13. Ride cancelled
    ↓
14. RideCancelledEvent (both rider & driver cancel by the extra attribute) (done)
    ↓
15. Publish Kafka

16. Driver accepts/rejects (done)
    ↓
17. DriverRideResponseEvent
    ↓
18. Publish Kafka
